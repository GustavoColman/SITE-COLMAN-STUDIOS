Colman studios

drawing has been present in my life since I was three years old, since I was a child, I created characters and put them in stories, drawing everything with a ballpoint pen in a sketchbook, and soon after, I showed everything to my classmates, who they followed these stories monthly, we took time during recess so everyone could look at my drawings, nowadays, this audience has become larger, with more than 1000 views on the reading platforms "tapas" and "fliptru", amon is the work which I am currently dedicated to, although I have left a long hiatus, Colman Studios has around 5000 followers on Facebook, with plans to expand to other platforms, here are some illustrations I made in recent years, they are a little outdated , but I intend to update this page with new works.

amon is a charater created by me for a dark fantasy webcomic with same name,The story follows the adventures of Amon, a mercenary with supernatural abilities, defeating hordes of demons and monstrosities, while becoming more involved in a sordid mystery.

The Ash Knight is a concept of a haunting, a knight who failed to defend his village and died in the middle of the flames, his desire for revenge gave life to this creature, imbued with fire, embers and hatred.

fatalis black blade redesign, a mod project that I'm working on, for the game monster hunter world, in which I intend to replace the design of the "fatalis black blade" weapon with my own model made by me, this is the reference drawing for the modeling

this is a monster concept based on the monster hunter game franchise, the idea was to create an original piscine wyvern

Kaisergate, Silva and Cassius, are the three protagonist characters of the current saga of a webcomic that I currently post on Facebook, normally focused on humor, but I decided to make a realistic version of the three to study shadows, Kaisergate is a character by the artist Eduardo Aguiar

owlsvald, a necromancer who is an enemy of Silva, one of his abilities is to transform into a form with owl-like characteristics, having large yellow eyes and long claws, his name is a pun on the animal from which he acquired characteristics through alchemical transmutation

Silva, the protagonist of the story, in this illustration, with a more realistic design, in this art I tried to do a color study and also a simple scene
